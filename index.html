About Me

I was born in Wuhan, China on November 24, 1991. My family and I migrated to the United States when I was one. First I lived in Oxford, Mississippi because my mother's friends lived there. Then I lived in Memphis, Tennessee and then San Diego, CA. Now I lived here in San Diego at Ivy Hill Drive for 20 years and made many friends during elementary school, middle school, and high school.

Connect with Me

After graduating from high school in June 2010 I attended college at UC Riverside. My major was in electrical engineering. At UC Riverside I enjoyed talking with roommates, dormmates, and other classmates about school related things. I graduated with a bachelors degree in December 2014. Afterwards I worked part time at SDSU as a research assistant for a NSF project from May 2015 to May 2016.
